About The Fairview Falcons

Fairview is home to one of the best national math compeititon team

The Fairview
Falcons.

Welcome to Fairview Middle School Math Club. Math club provides students opportunities to have fun in Math, strengthen their mathematical capabilities and interact with other students of similar interests. Math Club members get lot of opportunities to participate in Math Competitions. Competitions and the practice sessions help students to challenge themselves and develop their problem solving skills. Students who are in 6th grade and have an A or B in their Pre-Algebra course, students who are in 7th grade and have an A or B in their Algebra course and students who are in 8th grade and have an A or B in their Geometry course are eligible to join Math club.

We the Fairview Falcons have taken part in many competitions thoughout the course of history and we are deemed as one of the best math competition team in the nation. We taken part in many compeititon at local, state, and even national levels.
